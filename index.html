About Me

I'm a passionate game programming graduate from George Brown College, driven by a fervent love for game development. Hailing originally from Mexico and now calling Toronto, Canada my home, I bring a diverse perspective to my work. Proficient in languages like C++ and C#, my journey in game development has been enriched by my experiences with platforms such as Unity and Unreal Engine 5.

As the tech lead of Carpe Dino, I've honed my leadership skills and embraced the challenges of self-directed projects. My enthusiasm for crafting immersive experiences extends to level design, exemplified by my solo endeavor on Synchro, a project that fuels my ambition to collaborate with game designers and artists to elevate it to new heights for publication
